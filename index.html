Dear Colleagues,

We are very pleased to announce the upcoming SAFE LAPAROSCOPIC CHOLECYSTECTOMY PROGRAM, scheduled for July 21st 2024, at Jaipur in association with SMS Medical College and attached Hospitals Jaipur. This aims to expedite its scope in life sciences & health domains and provide excellent medical education. This course aims to provide comprehensive training in SAFE LAPAROSCOPIC CHOLECYSTECTOMY PROGRAM is designed for both practicing and aspiring surgeons. Our course will offer a unique opportunity to interact with experienced faculty members and enhance your proficiency in Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy Surgery. The sessions will cover a wide range of topics, from basic techniques to advanced procedures, with the primary objective of equipping all participants with the necessary skills to perform Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy Surgery proficiently.

Dr. Rajendra Mandia
Course Convenor
Sr. Professor
Department of General Surgery

DAY ONE SESSION:

  • Setup of Laparoscopic Surgery Unit, Equipment, Instrumentation & Endo vision
  • Sterilization and Maintenance of Laparoscopic Equipment
  • Electrocautery and Newer Energy source in Laparoscopy Anesthesia for Laparoscopic Surgery and complications of Pneumoperitoneum
  • Basics in Laparoscopic Suturing & Knotting
  • Ergonomics in laparoscopic surgery
  • Troubleshooting in Laparoscopy
  • Techniques of creation of Pneumoperitoneum (Step by Step with videos) and complication of Access
  • Video documentation and storage

  • *Panel Discussions:* Interactive discussions on the latest trends, challenges, and future directions in laparoscopic surgery
  • *Networking Opportunities:* Engage with peers, leaders, and innovators in the field
